Stokvela Rules

  • All payments must be made before or on the due date.
  • Late payments incur a penalty fee of 20% of the contribution amount.
  • Each member will receive the collected contributions in rotation, once per cycle.
  • Members must notify the group administrator in advance if unable to contribute on time.
  • Repeated late payments (3 or more) may result in removal from the Stokvela group.
  • All disputes must be resolved amicably with the group administrator.
